STATISTICS ON AGRICULTURE IN TANZANIA

ARABLE LAND

As of 2016, Tanzania had over 44 million hectares of arable land with only 33 percent utilization in cultivation. Land is a vital asset in ensuring food security. The nine main food crops in Tanzania are maize , sorghum, millet, rice, wheat, beans, cassava, potatoes, and bananas.

POPULATION DEPENDENT ON AGRICULTURE

The primary source of income for almost 70 percent of the population living in rural areas is agriculture.
