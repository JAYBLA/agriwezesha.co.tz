Agriwezesha

We are a Non Governmental Organization (NGO) founded and registered in 2019 under the Non-Government Organization Act of 2002.
Our main purpose is to work closely with small-holder farmers in Tanzania to enhance agricultural productivity by providing education/training and consultations on modern farming practices as well as improving farmers’ market connections.

Our Vision

To attain increased agricultural productivity and improved market connections for small-holder farmers in Tanzania.

Our Mission

To boost productivity and improve market connections in Tanzania by regarding the use of modern farming practices and technology which is affordable by small-holder farmers.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • 1To develop and implement programs which support small holder farmers on their lively hoods and the ecosystem
  • 2To initiate and implement environmental conservation programs in collaboration with small holder farmers.
  • 3To support small holder farmers on easily access of the markets for their produce.
  • 4To develop digital farmers’ services which aim at increasing the farmers’ working efficiency.
  • 5To initiate programs which guarantee food security to the farmers’ communities within Tanzania.

THINGS WE CAN DO FOR YOU

CONSULTATION

We are providing free consultations to small holder farmers on modern farming practices and farming technology. The farmers can get these services by visiting our office or by getting in touch with our technical experts through online.

TRAINING/EDUCATION

We are providing different trainings to smallholder farmers on modern farming practices and farming technology. We have practical training sessions that allow a farmer to gain hands-on experience. We also have online training programs that can be accessed freely by the farmers.

MARKET LINKAGE

We help small-holder farmers to get good and reliable markets for their products after harvest. We do this to increase their income and so later improve their standard of living.

Agroforestry Project

Agriwezesha is implementing tree planting project through the adaptation of Agroforestry practices and reforestation in the Morogoro Region in Mvomero In the year 202/23.

Agriwezesha planted 444,067 trees both fruit trees, this project is funded by Reforestation with the main purposes of conserving the environment and empowering economic well-being of the farms.

Our heartfelt thanks go to ReforestAction for supporting this project which created a lot of impact on the community as well as on the environment
